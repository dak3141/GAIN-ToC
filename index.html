Global Armenian Impact Network

Theory of Change

Vision

A resilient, connected, and empowered Global Armenian Nation that thrives in the 21st century through professional solidarity, cultural identity, innovation, and global collaboration.

Mission

To unite Armenians worldwide into a smart, inclusive, and dynamic universal network that fosters leadership, problem-solving, knowledge-sharing, and community resilience through AI-powered tools, mentorship, grants, education, and cultural engagement.

Problem Statement

The Armenian world is fragmented, facing technological disruption, cultural dilution, and geopolitical vulnerability. Diaspora talent and local capacity remain under-connected, with no single platform to unite our collective intelligence.

Our Strategic Pathway of Change

IF...

Armenians across sectors and geographies are connected on a values-based, trusted digital platform, and are provided with the tools, networks, and opportunities to collaborate...

THEN THEY WILL...

  • Develop leadership and resilience across generations
  • Exchange knowledge and mentor one another
  • Launch and support impactful initiatives
  • Strengthen cultural identity and solidarity

WHICH LEADS TO...

A vibrant global Armenian network capable of influencing change, uplifting communities, and advancing Armenia’s development.

Key Outcomes & Outputs

The measurable results of our strategic pathway.

Impact

A strong, self-sustaining global Armenian ecosystem of mutual support and national contribution.

Indicators:

  • Increase in cross-border collaborations.
  • Rise in diaspora-led investments and projects in Armenia and Armenian communities.

Outcomes

1. Strengthened Ties

Strengthened professional and personal ties among Armenians worldwide.

Indicators
  • % increase in cross-border connections via GAIN.
  • # of active mentorship/collaboration matches.
  • # of attendees in GAIN events.
  • % of users reporting stronger ties.
2. Global Leadership

More Armenians hold leadership positions on global decision-making arenas.

Indicators
  • # of GAIN alumni in executive roles or boards.
  • # of Armenians leading international coalitions facilitated by GAIN.
3. Tangible Contributions

Tangible contributions to Armenia’s innovation, resilience, and global influence.

Indicators
  • # of partnerships between diaspora and Armenia.
  • # of innovative solutions/startups supported by GAIN.
  • % of GAIN-funded projects reaching implementation.

Leadership development Academy delivered.

Strategic partnerships established with global institutions.

Activities

  • Design and launch the GAIN platform
  • Curate and match mentorship opportunities
  • Run leadership and cultural exchange programs
  • Provide grants for diaspora-Armenia projects
  • Host global networking and learning events
  • Integrate AI tools to personalize user journeys

Key Assumptions

  • Diaspora Armenians are willing to engage in long-term, trust-based collaboration.
  • There is demand for a curated, secure digital platform.
  • Cultural and professional common ground exists to foster unity across communities.

Risk and Mitigation

  • Low engagement: Run targeted outreach, offer incentives, build local ambassadors.
  • Fragmentation of efforts: Collaborate with existing orgs (e.g., AGBU, RepatArmenia).
  • Tech adoption barriers: Prioritize intuitive design, mobile-first functionality.

Stakeholders

  • Youth, students, and professionals in Armenia
  • Local and global Armenian organizations

Long-Term Vision of Success

GAIN becomes the trusted go-to platform for Armenians worldwide to connect, collaborate, and contribute. It cultivates a sense of shared destiny and empowers generations to protect, uplift, and lead the Armenian nation’s future.
